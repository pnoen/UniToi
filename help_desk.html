Help Desk

  • Do you have accounts for users?

    No, the only accounts are only for admins.


  • How do I rate a toilet?

    You can rate a toilet here. The page can also be found in the navigation bar.


  • How do I review a toilet?

    You can review a toilet here. The page can also be found in the navigation bar.


  • How can I get directions to the toilet?

    You click on the toilet on the map and sidebar will popup. Click on the arrow in the top left of the sidebar.


  • How can I get filter the toilet on the map?

    Click the top left button on the map to bring out the sidebar. Uncheck or check the toilets you wish to display.



  • If you have any other questions, feel free to contact us. Our details can be found on this page.
