Privacy Policy

Here at UniToi, we only ask for the location of the user to be able to locate a suitable toilet. It is at the user's discretion whether they wish to provide their location or not.

UniToi will store the email address and password of an Administrator account. However the website can be used to locate a suitable bathroom without supplying any personal information.
